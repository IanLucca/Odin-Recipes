Munguzá

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 – 110 oz can hominy, drained
  • 1 ½ Cup whole milk
  • 1 Cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • ½ Tsp. ground cinnamon
  • ½ Tsp. ground clove
  • ¼ Cup sugar
  • ¼ Tsp. salt​
  • Condensed milk, optional

Steps

  1. Place hominy, milk, coconut milk, spices, salt, and sugar in a saucepan and simmer over medium heat until thickened, about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Optional: Serve with a drizzle of condensed milk.
